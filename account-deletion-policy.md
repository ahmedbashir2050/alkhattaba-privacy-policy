# Account Deletion Policy

**Last Updated: June 2026**

## User Rights

Users have the right to permanently delete their accounts and associated data.

## How to Delete an Account

Inside the Application:

1. Open Settings.
2. Select Delete Account.
3. Confirm deletion.

Or contact:

support@alkhattaba.com

## What Happens After Deletion

Upon deletion:

- Profile becomes inaccessible.
- Personal information is removed from public view.
- Associated content may be anonymized.

## Data Retention

Certain records may be temporarily retained for:

- Legal compliance
- Fraud prevention
- Security investigations

## Processing Time

Deletion requests are generally processed within 30 days.
