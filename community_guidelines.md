# Community Guidelines

**Last Updated: June 2026**

## Our Mission

AlKhattaba aims to provide a respectful, safe, and trustworthy environment for individuals seeking marriage.

## Respect Other Members

Always:

- Be respectful.
- Be honest.
- Communicate appropriately.
- Respect personal boundaries.

## Prohibited Content

The following are strictly prohibited:

- Nudity
- Pornographic content
- Sexual exploitation
- Hate speech
- Racism
- Harassment
- Threats
- Violence
- Fraud
- Illegal activities

## Authentic Profiles

Users must:

- Use genuine photos.
- Provide accurate information.
- Represent themselves honestly.

Fake profiles may be permanently removed.

## Safety Recommendations

Users should:

- Protect personal information.
- Report suspicious behavior.
- Exercise caution when meeting others.
- Avoid sharing financial information.

## Reporting Violations

Users can report violations through the application.

Reports are reviewed by moderators.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warnings
- Temporary restrictions
- Account suspension
- Permanent removal
